About Us

Liability insurance covers legal costs and payouts for which the insured party would be found liable. Provisions not covered include Intentional damage, contractual liabilities, and criminal prosecution. Liability insurance is often required for automotive insurance policies, product manufacturers, and anyone who practices medicine or law. Personal liability, Product and Public Liability, workers' compensation, and commercial liability are types of liability insurance.
